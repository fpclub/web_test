04 : Flying School

Learn To Fly

You can't just wake up in the morning and pretend to fly.

Every pig will have to attend the Flying school and if he survives training he will indeed be a fully fledged Flying pig! .

So how does it work?

Every day you'll have the chance to mint an unlimited amount of pigs for a duration of 10 days. During these days the pigs will learn to fly, only 10k will survive training, the more you mint the higher the chances one of your candidates graduates.

The Mint

Minting will be through our website, price still to be determined but currecny will be Eth.

We will keep the mint unlimited in a "raffle" style in order to keep gas prices down and make the minting process all the more fun
