Flying Pigs Club

Flying Pigs Club originated from the degenerated minds of 3 young developers as a representation of the madness of our current times. We now live in a world where everything is possible, where crypto currencicies can take us to the moon, where even pigs... CAN FLY! .

The Art

The series constists of 10k randomly generated pigs with a different attributes such as wings (obviously),headgear,expression etc. There will also be some uniques and rare pieces to look out for!
